üéØ GOAL

The goal of an alliance (alliance: 3 teams in one, 3x3 football), which consists of 3 drone balls, is to score as many goals as possible against the opposing team.

üë• TEAM ‚Äì COACH

  • Participation: Team-based, not individual
  • Team Size: 2-3 members per team
  • Robot Athlete Technician: Each team designates up to 1 technician
  • Access: Only technician allowed in waiting/competition area
  • Age Requirement: All members must be at least 10 years old
  • Coach: Must be at least 20 years old
  • Assistant Requirement: 1 assistant for every 3 teams registered
  • Drone Ball Limit: Only one drone ball per team; no changing during competition
  • No Sharing: Teams cannot share the same Drone Ball
  • Emergency Replacement: Only with Head Judge permission for serious technical issues

ü§ù ALLIANCES

  • Alliance Formation: 3 teams = 1 alliance
  • Drone Balls: Each team has only 1 drone ball
  • Match Format: 2 opposing alliances compete (3v3)
  • Determination: Alliances determined by system after registration ends
  • Notification: Teams informed via email by organizing committee
  • Missing Teams: If 1 team doesn't show, alliance continues with 2 drone balls
  • 2 Missing Teams: Sole team competes without replacements
  • Disqualification: If alliance disqualified, opposing alliance awarded victory

ü©π DRONE BALL INJURY

A Drone Ball is considered injured when:

  • A part has been disassembled
  • It remains stationary (lost connection with controller)

Injury Procedure:

  • Injured drone ball remains on ground until 4-minute match completed
  • Team can repair after match ends for next game
  • All Drones Injured: Referee stops match, allows 10 minutes for repairs
  • After 10 minutes: Match resumes even if only 1 drone ball returns
  • Other 2 players not allowed to enter field if not ready
